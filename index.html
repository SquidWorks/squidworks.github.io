What is Squid Works?

We are a research and developemnt shop that works to develop and enhance technical capabilities at the tactical level using open source software and COTS hardware. We will provide free R&D to anyone who asks, free of charge, on any subject. Everything we do is unclass, but we do not advertise who we work for, and any member of any organization can send us a request. We don't care about rank, there is no drawn out approval process or contracting requirements, just email us at squidworks@protonmail.com to get started.

If you want to learn more about us, visit our About page.



How it Works

We make the process as simple as possible: someone emails us an idea, we contact them to clarify and clarify design requirements, then we assign the project to one or more of our team members. This simplified development process optimizes our speed, allowing us to make us much of an impact as possible.
