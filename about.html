About Squid Works

I created this site because while teaching yourself how to be a hacker is hard, teaching yourself how to be a hacker the right way is even harder.

Squid Works is the child of a decade of attempting to find resources online to expand my knowledge. There is no lack of resources, but rather, there is a lack of flow, a lack of coherent frameworks and training plans. And while going a rambling on the internet works for some people, it doesn't work for most of us. I instantly gravitated towards Hack Forums and Hack This Site. Plenty of others did as well, but significantly more simply quit because they thought they didn't know enough and could never learn all the material out there.

The simple fact of the matter is that noone can even approach knowing a fraction of what there is to know about this domain, however, this site aims to give students the tools required to navigate anything thrown at them. By repeatedly stressing that students are not expected to show up being elite hackers, or to leave as elite hackers, this site attempts to fill the gap between "I want to cyber" and "I cyber".

All we hope to do here is to provide guidance for the first 6 months of your journey into cyberspace, after that, we hope to have given you the tools, techniques, and hacker mentality required to address any problem that comes your way.

How It Works

How the Squid Works process works.

  • Right Now

    You Have A Problem

    Or perhaps a list of problems. Or maybe they are just ideas. No matter what, there are things you need someone to work on. That's where we come in.

  • 5 Minutes from Now

    You Fill Out and Submit a Project Request

    Email us at squidworks@protonmail.com to get started.

  • Tomorrow

    We Recieve Your Request

    We check our email and see your request. We then categorize the project based on skills required, estimated time, and priority level. Once we do that, we begin reaching out to our team to see who is able to take on the project.

  • 2 Days from Now

    We Assign a Researcher To Your Project

    By now we have one of our team members at work on your project. After researching the subject so they have a better idea of what they are being asked to do, they work through the project requirements and develop a plan for moving forward according to the project's timeline.

  • 3 Days From Now

    Our Researcher Reaches Out

    We don't want to waste any of our time or yours, so we will reach out to the requesting party to discuss the project and go over any parts that need further clarification. By smoothing out questions ahead of time we can make sure that the finished project will fulfill the requirements of what is needed.

  • R&D Time

    Our Researchers Work on Your Project

    Based off of the timeline outlined during the planning stages, our team members will work to complete your project. This can take any length of time, but we will work to get everything done as quickly as possible, without failing any of our classes.

  • Completion

    We Submit our Finished Project

    Once the project is complete we forward the product back to the requesting party. We then begin the process of tuning the product until it fits the exact operational need it was built for. We are not done working on it until the project has been adopted by your in-house developers or it no longer needs to be worked on.
